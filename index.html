Kelowna to Big White: Drive vs. Shuttle

🚗 Driving (Rental/Personal)

Summary:

Driving offers the most flexibility. The trip takes approximately 1 hour each way (60 km). A 4WD or AWD vehicle with winter tires is highly recommended as the road conditions can be challenging.

Pros:
  • Complete control over your schedule.
  • Ability to bring all your gear easily.
  • Can stop for supplies on the way back.
Cons:
  • Requires a designated driver.
  • Fuel and potential rental costs.
  • Navigating winter mountain roads.
  • Parking at Big White can be limited.

🚌 Big White Shuttle

Summary:

Multiple shuttle services run daily routes between Kelowna and Big White. It's a convenient, stress-free option that drops you right at the village centre. Booking in advance is essential.

Pros:
  • No need to worry about winter driving.
  • Everyone in the group can relax.
  • No parking hassles at the resort.
  • Direct drop-off at the main village.
Cons:
  • Fixed departure and return times.
  • Can be costly for a large group.
  • Less flexibility for off-schedule activities.

Common Shuttle Pickup Locations

  • Kelowna Int'l Airport (YLW)
  • Major Downtown Hotels
  • Orchard Park Mall
  • West Kelowna Hubs
